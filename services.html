Services

Modular RNA-seq support from study design to final figure package.

Choose a focused service for one step of the analysis or combine several into a full end-to-end workflow for your project.

Most projects are customized after a short intake discussion covering sample type, number of groups, metadata availability, and publication goals.

1. Project setup and experimental review

  • Study design check before analysis begins.
  • Advice on metadata structure, contrasts, and replication.
  • Clarification of questions best answered with RNA-seq.

2. QC and preprocessing review

  • FastQC-style quality interpretation and sample-level flags.
  • Assessment of outliers, duplication patterns, and alignment summaries.
  • Recommendations before downstream testing.

3. Differential expression analysis

  • Group comparisons matched to your design.
  • Fold-change and adjusted p-value based prioritization.
  • Annotated gene lists for downstream review.

4. Enrichment and pathway interpretation

  • GO, KEGG, Hallmark, or custom gene set analysis.
  • Biological theme extraction for presentation and manuscript use.
  • Focused interpretation around disease, treatment, or cell-state questions.

5. Visualization and reporting

  • Heatmaps, PCA plots, volcano plots, dot plots, and summary panels.
  • Organized figure files for slides and publications.
  • Methods-ready notes describing the workflow and thresholds used.

6. Consultation and revision rounds

  • Follow-up discussion after delivery.
  • Clarifications for coauthors, supervisors, or collaborators.
  • Reasonable revisions when the scientific question evolves.
